Skybreaker

Introduction

The Skybreaker is a unique and powerful entity created by the 'Creator of Everything'. As an ancient and invulnerable being, the Skybreaker holds immense strength and possesses a purpose that extends beyond ordinary existence.

Background

Created to protect the Creator of Everything from the potential consequences of the vast and intricate creation of 'everything', the Skybreaker is one of the most formidable beings across all realms. Although its true nature was concealed through memory loss, it plays a crucial role in maintaining the balance of existence.

Powers and Abilities

The Skybreaker, known for its incredible physical power, surpasses many other entities in strength. It is capable of lifting objects of unimaginable weight and overcoming challenges that span across the infinite multiverse. Its abilities are purely physical, not relying on magic or supernatural forces.

Role and Significance

Despite its extraordinary powers, the Skybreaker lives a seemingly ordinary life as a household worker, unaware of its true identity. This duality highlights the complexity of its existence and the balance between mundane life and cosmic responsibility.
